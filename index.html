Bala & Nirmal Marathon Pigeons

Welcome to website of Marathon Pigeons, We posses various strains of extreme long distance throughbred pigeons with strong racing background. These european strains are started proving in our weather conditions with top velocities. Most of our pigeons are purchased from top barcelona flyers.

We Bala & Nirmal

Myself (Nirmalraj Durai) and my brother (Balachandran Durai), working as a IT Professional & passionate on racing pigeons since childhood. To keep a strong performing pigeons we have done an extensive research and settled with european pigeons.
